Deal Cards.

Want to make content areas more noticeable? Cards are one of the more well known trends in responsive design. Cards can be singular, grouped together or equal height. There are many different layout and content options.

Cards are a good way to display content composed of different types of objects. They’re well-suited for presenting similar objects whose size or supported actions vary, like headings and photos with captions.

Use card groups to render equal height cards without gutters between the cards. Use .card-deck for cards that aren’t attached to each another, and thanks to flexbox we now have equal height cards! Clear, concise and bold.
